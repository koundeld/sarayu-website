Why Sarayu
exists.

After years spent in the relentless pace of the city, we chose to build something enduring — a place where the land sets the rhythm and hospitality is felt rather than performed.

This retreat is called Sarayu — named after the ancient river celebrated in Indian tradition for its quiet strength and enduring presence. A river that has shaped entire landscapes without ever announcing itself.

✦

A river does not rush. It carries depth without noise. It shapes with patience, not force. That philosophy defines every decision made here — from the materials we chose, to the number of cottages we built.

Our cottages are hand-built using mud, stone, wood, and lime. Materials that breathe. That regulate temperature without mechanical help. That age into the landscape rather than fighting it. Local craftsmen brought generational knowledge to every wall. Nothing here was assembled. Everything was made.

Sarayu is not a high-volume resort. It is an intimate mountain estate — two cottages, one orchard, one stream, and uninterrupted Himalayan sky.

सरयू — The Name

Sarayu (Sanskrit: सरयू) is an ancient river referenced in Indian scripture — revered for its clarity, constancy, and quiet depth. It does not demand attention. Its presence is simply felt. We chose this name because we believe a retreat should do the same.
