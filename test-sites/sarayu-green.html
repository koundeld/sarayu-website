Our Story

Why
Sarayu

✦

This retreat is named after the ancient river known for its quiet strength and enduring presence. Like a river, Sarayu flows with steadiness — never hurried, never excessive.

After years in the rat race, we chose to build something enduring — a place where craftsmanship, land, and hospitality meet with restraint and refinement.

Sarayu is not a high-volume resort. It is an intimate mountain estate where every detail reflects our belief that true luxury lies in space, silence, and thoughtful execution.

How We Build

Crafted from the Earth

Mud, stone, wood, and lime — materials that breathe, regulate temperature naturally, and age with character.

01

Natural Materials

Mud, stone, wood, and lime — each chosen for how they breathe, insulate, and age. Cement used only where structurally essential.

02

Local Craftsmanship

Every wall is hand-built by local artisans carrying generational knowledge. This is architecture with permanence — built to endure.

03

Deliberate Phases

Construction unfolds in measured stages, respecting the land's natural rhythm. Growth that never compromises character.
