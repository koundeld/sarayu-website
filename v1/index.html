Our Story

Built with
intention,
for the mindful.

Sarayu is an intimate retreat carved into a working apple orchard in Manali — handcrafted using recycled natural materials, local sourcing, and eco-conscious practices that leave the landscape undisturbed.

Here, luxury is not excess. It is space, silence, and care. Two private cottages. A stream on one side. An orchard on the other.
